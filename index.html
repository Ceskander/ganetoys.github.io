Description

Gane Toys introduces children ages 2-5 to foreign languages using a screen-less platform. With voice activation, our stuffed animals interact with children through storytelling, songs, and games to prepare them for our globalized world.

Gane Toys will give future generations the foundations of conversational language learning and a new best friend. Through the use of voice recognition, our stuffed animals will orally translate words and phrases that a child uses daily and can build into deeper conversational language as the child learns.
